Essential Tips for Your Safety

🧠 Be Aware: Always stay alert and aware of your surroundings, especially when alone.
📲 Keep Phone Ready: Make sure your phone is fully charged and emergency contacts are saved.
🗣️ Trust Your Instincts: If something feels off, trust your gut and take action.
🚶 Share Route: Inform a trusted friend or family member about your travel route and ETA.
